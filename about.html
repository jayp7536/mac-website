About MAC @ Pitt



The Minority Association in Computing nurtures diversity and inclusion in the technology field while more specifically creating community for the underrepresented students of Pitt’s School of Computing and Information through professional, social, educational, and other engagements.


Our mission is to build a strong community where every student feels welcome and has the resources to succeed in computer science and related fields.
